Step 1. Have World of Horror (https://store.steampowered.com/app/913740/WORLD_OF_HORROR/)

Step 2. Right click on the game in your steam library, select Manage>Browse Local Files

Step 3. Have the "JLP World of Horror Expansion" Zip file open, click and drag the files from the zip file into the World of Horror files

Step 4. Have fun!
